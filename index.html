Shop restrictions...



Due to the current global circumstances regarding Covid-19... we have implemented a number of changes in The Coffee Lounge... to keep you and ourselves safe.

We now require ALL customers to wear a mask or face covering when in The Lounge... and not to remove it until they leave. We will also require you to 'Check In' on the NHS Covid-19 App, or provide us with your name and telephone number.

We are currently operating as TAKEAWAY or CLICK & COLLECT only.

Please also note... social distancing measures in place... and we would ask all of our customers to pay attention to and adhere to any instructions and signage when in store.
